40ants/cl-hamcrest
It is implementation of [Hamcrest](http://hamcrest.org) idea in Common Lisp. It simplifes unittests and make them more readable. Hamcrest uses idea of pattern-matching, to construct matchers from different pieces and to apply them to the data. Works nicely with Fukamachi's [Prove](https://github.com/fukamachi/prove>) and [Rove](https://github.com/fukamachi/rove>) unittesting frameworks, but could be extended to support other frameworks.
